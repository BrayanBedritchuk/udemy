Default -> 3D View Port Cube, Camara and Lamp

Hide the tools: T
Hide the other menus: Shift + Space

Navigation: Click with the mouse wheel and drag

Fix camera: 1, 3, 7 

Select object: Right click

Move object: G

Scale object: S

Rotate: R

Alt + G + S + R: Clear movement, scale and rotation
